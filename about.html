About Grizzly Parrot Trading

Grizzly Parrot Trading is an educational site focused on explaining how futures markets actually work. The goal is simple: clear explanations of contracts, liquidity, execution, risk, and market structure.

A large amount of trading content online focuses on predictions, shortcuts, and marketing. This site takes a different approach. The focus here is understanding the mechanics that drive price movement so traders can make better decisions.

What This Site Covers

  • Futures contract mechanics
  • Currency and metals futures
  • Liquidity and market structure
  • Risk, drawdown, and trading survival
  • Platform guides for NinjaTrader and Tradovate
  • Prop firm rules and common evaluation traps

Why This Site Exists

Most traders fail because they do not fully understand the markets they trade. Liquidity shifts, session behavior, contract structure, and risk dynamics all influence outcomes. When those mechanics are misunderstood, mistakes compound quickly.

This site exists to explain those mechanics in a way that is practical, readable, and useful while someone is actually looking at a chart.

The Grizzly Parrot Approach

The material on this site focuses on direct explanations rather than predictions. Some topics only require a short article, while others require deeper breakdowns. The objective is not to sound impressive. The objective is clarity.

If a concept helps traders understand how futures markets function, it belongs here.

Books And Long-Form Work

Grizzly Parrot Trading is published by Kyle Parrott. In addition to the educational articles on this site, Kyle Parrott is also the author of several books that explore futures trading frameworks and market behavior in greater depth.

Current titles include Probabilistic Execution and Currency Market Structure – Volume I. The articles on this site explain individual concepts and mechanics, while the books connect those ideas into broader frameworks for understanding market structure, execution, and risk.
